Marketing Capstone
MKTG:4500

Office Depot
Marketing Analysis

Alongside five other individuals, we explored office depots marketing throughout history as well as the current standing and what changes they could make using factors such as Market Rejuvenation, Management Audits, Segmetation and Positioning, Brand Extensions and more!
