2021-2024

Walk It Out

President
Vice President of Marketing
Graphic Designer

Walk It Out is the largest Multicultural Organization at the University of Iowa. By utilizing dance, fashion, music and performance, Walk It Out aims to bridge the gap between the cultural groups on campus and its PDI. Walk It Out gives minority students the platform the unique marvels the respective culture possess. With over 150 members, I had the honor to serve Walk It Out as the 2024 President and showcasing the beauty of community and culture to over 800 individuals in the Iowa City Community.

Women in Business

To kickstart my Marketing studies, I took part in a project as the company P&G to rejuvenate their popular product, the Gillete Razor.
