Education

I am a soon to be graduate from the University of Iowa. Studying Marketing and Business Analytics in the Tippie College of Business with a Minor in Psychology. I also got the honor to study abroad through the CIMBA program in Italy where I got to learn marketing and finance from professors across the nation.
