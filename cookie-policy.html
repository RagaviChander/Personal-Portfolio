Cookie Policy

This website uses cookies to ensure you get the best experience on our website.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored by your web browser when you use websites. They contain basic information about your internet use, but do not include personal information.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • To improve the functionality of our website.
  • To analyze how users interact with our website.
  • To personalize content and ads.
  • To provide social media features.

Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed. If you do this, however, you may have to manually adjust some preferences every time you visit a site and some services and functionalities may not work.
